Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Sudoku Offline (“the app”) is an offline-first Sudoku puzzle app. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Summary

  • No account is required.
  • Your puzzles, progress, stats, and settings are stored on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • The app is designed to work without an internet connection.

Information stored on your device

The app stores data locally on your phone or tablet, including:

  • Game progress (board state, notes, timer, mistakes, hints used)
  • Daily puzzle progress and streak-related records
  • Completion stats and history
  • App settings (for example theme, haptics, and gameplay preferences)
  • Premium or “Remove Ads” entitlement state, when applicable

This data stays on your device unless you delete the app or clear app data. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

Information we do not collect directly

We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

Analytics

The app may record anonymous usage events locally (for example puzzle started, puzzle completed, or setting changed) to help improve the product. In current versions, these events are queued on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. If we add optional online analytics in a future update, this policy will be updated to describe what is collected and how you can control it.

Advertising

Sudoku Offline does not show banner or interstitial ads during gameplay. We use Google AdMob to deliver two kinds of non-intrusive ads: optional rewarded ads, which appear only when you choose to watch one for a clear benefit (for example an extra hint), and native ads on non-gameplay surfaces such as the puzzle-completion screen.

Google AdMob may collect device and usage data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google’s advertising policies. The app requests non-personalized ads. On iOS, AdMob’s access to the device advertising identifier is governed by Apple’s App Tracking Transparency, and you can change your choice at any time in iOS Settings. Players who purchase “Remove Ads” are not shown native ads and receive the rewarded perks without watching an ad.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store). We receive confirmation of purchase status so premium features can work offline. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Children

The app is a general-audience puzzle game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact
